Action plan
Where to start

Five actions ordered by impact. The first three can be activated in under 2 weeks and generate immediate visibility with no ad spend required.

01
Google Business Profile — Optimise the profile
The profile already exists — that's a real advantage. Now it needs to be optimised: quality photos of properties, a complete description of services, systematic responses to reviews and active collection of new ones. Every additional review directly improves local rankings.
Top priority
02
Idealista + Fotocasa — Publish listings
The two leading property portals in Spain. Each listing published with professional photos and bilingual descriptions (ES + EN) generates qualified leads without needing organic traffic.
Top priority
03
Instagram Business — Visual content of properties
Open profile + first 12 posts featuring the most attractive properties. Expat buyers use Instagram as a shop window before contacting an agency. Bilingual content EN/ES.
Within 2 weeks
04
SEO — Optimisation and editorial content
The current site has wide room for SEO improvement. With on-page optimisation for priority areas (Cabo Roig, Villamartín, La Zenia) and a blog with evergreen content (buying property in Spain, NIE, taxes...) stable organic rankings can be built over time.
Within 30-60 days
05
Review collection system — Building reputation from scratch
Every satisfied client is a review opportunity. An automated post-sale/rental process turns each transaction into public credibility on Google and Idealista.
Long-term foundation
